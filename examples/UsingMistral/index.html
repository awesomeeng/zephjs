Toggle Box
<mistral-toggle-box>
A box that hides its body content based on a toggle label. Sometimes called a collapse/expand box or panel.
<mistral-toggle-box label="Some Colorful Boxes">     <div style="width: 50px; height: 50px; background: red; border: 1px solid black; margin: 5px;"></div>     <div style="width: 50px; height: 50px; background: green; border: 1px solid black; margin: 5px;"></div>     <div style="width: 50px; height: 50px; background: blue; border: 1px solid black; margin: 5px;"></div> </mistral-toggle-box>

Text Field
Text Field
<mistral-text-field>
A wrappers around text field input[type=text] elements with support for labels, placeholders, required flag, disabled flag, and errors. Supports all attributes that input[type=text] supports for the associated type.
<mistral-text-field name="example"></mistral-text-field>
<mistral-text-field name="example" disabled></mistral-text-field>
<mistral-text-field name="example" placeholder="some placeholder text"></mistral-text-field>
<mistral-text-field name="example" required></mistral-text-field>
<mistral-text-field name="example" type="text" error="some error text"></mistral-text-field>
